# Unit 1: Heat
**Class 6 · Science · Term II (Tamil Nadu State Board) · Source PDF pages 7-21**
Source: `ReferenceBook/6th_Science_Term_II_EM - www.tntextbooks.in.pdf`

## Key concepts extracted (Intake stage)

- Sources of heat: sun, friction, combustion, electricity
- Heat = total kinetic energy of the constituent particles (molecules) of an object; SI unit joule
- Hot/cold objects and the unreliability of touch; temperature as the precise measurement
- Temperature: SI unit kelvin; Celsius and Fahrenheit also used
- Heat vs. temperature: temperature relates to average molecular speed; heat also depends on
  the number of molecules present
- Thermal contact and thermal equilibrium: heat flows hot -> cold until temperatures equalize
- Thermal expansion: linear/area/volume expansion on heating, contraction on cooling
- Uses of thermal expansion: gaps in railway tracks and bridge joints, iron rim shrink-fitted onto
  wooden wheels, borosilicate (pyrex) glass resisting expansion cracks
